From sustainability-inclined policies to 3D pharmaceuticals, what's not to love?

I love working on new ideas with people who are equally excited about them.
When I'm not working on any of my projects, you can often find me

My background

I'm currently a Computational Systems and Biology Major at UCLA. There's much to explore! From the Biomedical Engineering Society's Build Team to International Bruins in Business and much more.

I attended University of Toronto Schools, where I was involved with HOSA, DECA, Classics, Art Club, and our student publications.
Being involved with a wide range of areas played a key role in developing my love for approaching challenges from creative perspectives. My high school years are also where I began truly developing an awareness of what I could do for my community: from working with local businesses, schools, and libraries to run case competitions for other students, to volunteering at events such as our Winter Festivals.
